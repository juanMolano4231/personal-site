Top animes

Serial Experiments Lain

It's about Lain Iwakura discovering the Wired, a network that connects people remotely, and slowly losing her sense of self the longer she uses it.

And also a bunch of people start to suicide to leave this fake world and live eternally in the Wired, the more real world.

I really like it and think it's the best anime I've watched yet, because it touches on a lot of topics I find very interesting, like philosophy and cybernetics.

Maoujou de Oyasumi

I really like this one, it's about Hime-sama whose name is Aurora (I didn't know) getting kidnapped by demons and kept captive in their castle, however she does not really care at all and passes her days breaking out of her cell and searching for things to make her sleep better.

No Game No Life

It's got an incredibly good premise, and although I don't entirely like the humor, it's still really cool.

It revolves around Sora and Shiro, the two siblings that beat an otherwordly god at chess and thus get invited to his world, Disboard.

In this world a horrible war occured, which managed to fatigue the stars and dry the oceans, so when Tet ended up "winning" the war, he forbade bodily harm; however, this didn't get rid of conflict, so he made it an immutable rule of the world for all conflicts to be resolved in an absolutely binding game, as long as the parties agreed beforehand.

Here's the interesting part, you can cheat as long as you don't get caught.

Sousou no Frieren

Frieren is an longevous elf that once helped in the war against demon-kind and defeated the demon king with her party, 80 years ago.

Because of being an elf, she outlives her human friends, and because she knows that, she decides not to get too attached to them, only to end up regretting not enjoying their time together more.

She then decides to start a journey towards a land where the dead rest, so that she can talk with them again, however it'll take her long years, where she'll make new human friends and this time won't miss the chance to enjoy the journey.

Youjo Senki

The protagonist works in a company, his job is firing people, but because he's very sadistic he has no problem breaking the bad news on a weekly basis.

However, one day, while waiting for the train, one of the people he fired, and ended up with his life ruined, sneaks up on him and pushes him to the tracks while the train comes at full speed.

But then, time stops and he floats, fixed in place, over the tracks, with the train about to hit him, and a voice speaks.

It says it is God and it asks the protag if he believes in him now, but he refuses, he says the "Entity X" could very well be something powerful, but not God itself.

Self proclaimed God gets very offended, and says that he will challenge this guy to see for how long he can keep sceptic.

Time resumes and he gets killed by the train, only to wake up as a baby, in a different world.

TL;DR he's a girl now and is joining the military of this country that's basically a magic fantasy version of Germany during WWI. "God" will give her enormous magical power to destroy her enemies during battle, but the cost is having to pray.

I really like Selphius's german cover of the ending song, 'Los Los Los'.
